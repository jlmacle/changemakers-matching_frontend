Adding a local self-signed certificate
- Powershell admin:
    * New-SelfSignedCertificate -DnsName "localhost" -CertStoreLocation "cert:\LocalMachine\My" -NotAfter (Get-Date).AddYears(100)
    * certlm.msc
- localhost certificate moved from "Personal" to "Trusted Root Certification Authorities"
